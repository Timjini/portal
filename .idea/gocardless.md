"Checkout" process:

Step 1: Create the Billing Request. You now have a BRQ... ID.

Step 2 (The Missing Step): Create a Billing Request Flow. This generates a URL.

Step 3 (The Customer Step): You send that URL to your customer. They click it, see a GoCardless page, enter their bank details, and click "Confirm."

Step 4 (The Completion): GoCardless redirects them back to your site, and the status of your request moves from pending to fulfilled.
